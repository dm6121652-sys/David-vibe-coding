Our Story & Purpose

Beauty, taught and applied with intention

Beauty by Vicky exists for the woman of substance — the one with somewhere important to be, and every reason to feel unshakeable getting there. We don't just apply makeup. We study your face, listen to your day, and build a look that holds up under lights, tears, dancing, and time.

Every session doubles as a small beauty lesson. We want you to leave not just glowing, but understanding why it works — the products, the technique, the order of operations — so confidence travels home with you, not just the makeup.

Our Vision

To become one of the most trusted and recognisable luxury makeup brands in Nigeria — and eventually internationally — known for exceptional artistry, professionalism, and an unforgettable client experience.
